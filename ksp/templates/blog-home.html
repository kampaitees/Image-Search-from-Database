IS LAW CATCHING UP?

Given pace of digital technology, the intensity and all pervasiveness of cyber space pushing ahead unabated, one needs to pause and think as to where is the legal framework to support it. To have a check on the seemingly unruled and unregulated IT behemoths and to provide succour to the ongoing loot of data worldwide. The emerging / disruptive technologies are bound to change the world beyond recognition and the law as the sentinel of the masses has to emerge in the same manner as disruptive and emerging technologies have.

The court software / algorithm would give the acceptance after going through fed parameters, physical intervention if required, the summons / warrants would be served online with copies to all process authorities with defined roles, the trial to be conducted through video, on acceptable gadgets / programs, the collation – the narratives automatically happening with ensuing orders on every date of hearing / trail and finally the the essential facts / findings with rudimentary evaluation would be put up for the final judgement before the presiding judge. Undeniably, legal / procedural enablements need to happen.

Technically it can happen forthwith. The other bottlenecks have still to be ironed out, it would be paradigm shift for all stakeholders in all their operations. It can be extrapolated to other laws in whatever area vis a vis the present level of digital technology. We have to reach this operational point at the earliest to make our legal onslaught on the disruptive technologies impact on systems / human life. The world is at the crossroads of technology and law.

LAW HAS TO ALIGN WITH TECHNOLOGY TO EVOLVE THE NEW DIGITAL AGE RULE OF LAW FRAMEWORK.

REFLECTED GLORYs

Basking in somebody else’s glory is one of the core behavioural traits of an Indian, the best way be finds to introduce himself is by taking the names of few successful persons who would be known to the person he is meeting. The next stage would be to prove his personal connect with that ‘mythical person’, who at best be would have barely known and more often than not would have just heard of him. With such behavioural trait if you really happen to be close; familial or otherwise to a person of glory and success, we can well imagine how most such connected people would bask in reflected glory and if given a chance might even end up exploiting it to the fullest.

If you analyse the most of the everyday conversations in this country, the one common and predominant thread would for sure be the standard streak of reflected glory as a part of our culture. The family genealogy with one’s having a few glorious ones are at full display whatever might be the occasion and however much long the connection would be. Reflected glory in simple terms means fame or approval achieved through association with someone else rather than through one’s own efforts.

With this mindset most of even the business conversations revolve around the person or persons whose glory the practitioner has decided to exploit and the crux turns out to be asking for a favourable attitude or a decision you are supposed to make. The creation and subsistence of a coterie or a clique around such persons is the epitome of this thought process. The unfortunate part is that most of times in these cases, the person with success or power or both, also becomes a party to the nefarious engagements.

Finding happiness in someone’s success and glory is the most natural of human tendencies though this seems to be getting decimated these days but making a business out of it, even with the knowledge of that person is a criminal act. The reference to successful projects or out of the context exaggeration of it or citing unwarranted academic or professional achievements also fall in the same genre.

BUT FOR THE TRUE CREDENTIALS REQUIRED FOR THE JOB OR THE PROFESSIONAL ENGAGEMENT, REFLECTED GLORY IS BANDIED.

ORGANIC GROWTH Vs CRITICAL MASS

Whether it’s an individual or a business enterprise or for that matter any institution or an organisation, the simplest way of its creation and development has been organic growth, not expressed in management terminology. Organic growth is time taking and but its success in creating robust institutions / organisations is higher. Nonetheless, other business enabling requirements and good / sustained leadership remains the key in any volume related model. In contrast is the critical mass model, created reasonably big at the outset itself and then scaled.

Critical Mass model presupposes the successful collation / collaboration of skills / competencies, visionary leadership and funding to start at operational levels. One simple example would explain it all; we have all heard of schools starting from one or two teachers and five / ten students growing up into massively successful ones in 15 / 20 years time. Robust and rock solid, built brick and brick. In contrast a modern day school with hundreds of crores of funding, all infrastructure, hiring of teachers as per norms / business model and staring with 1000/2000 students and hitting the roof in just two / three years. This is the critical mass model.

So is the case of businesses. The Haldiram or WIipro Way don’t seem to be the favoured models, Flipkarts, Udaans and Cult Fits are. The gestation period is to be bridged as much as one can, so that it becomes economically viable, ensuring very high returns in a short time. Might be the IT behemoths model is propelling this model of Critical Mass model growth. The immense opportunities of the digital age and the capability of fund raising lies behind the creation of big businesses right from the word go. In reality, the Google and Apple have grown the organic way, though abridged, compared to the creation of earlier mammoth business enterprises.

Whatever might be the route, critical mass has to happen, for business to move to a different level. For educational institutions organic growth model still seems worthwhile. The emotional connect with the organisation is another key factor, long duration familiarity binds it further. Enduring business entity or institution is the final destination.

TRUE CRITICAL MASS TRANSFORMS BUSINESS TO A DIFFERENT GROWTH TRAJECTORY.
